My World Famous Tortilla Recipe

NOW, the moment you have all been waiting for, the scrumptious tortilla recipe!

So, the needed materials are:

  • A tortilla
  • Your two favorite pieces of ham
  • Some bellpepper that is specifically red, not green
  • French fries perchance

Steps:

  1. Grab your tortilla
  2. Lay it flat on the table of your choice (plastic table works)
  3. Place pieces of ham and place vertically on tortilla
  4. Carefully lay french fries across the ham
  5. Spread out red bellpeppers across french fries

Viola!

Your meal can now be enjoyed, tastes better when sitting on a plastic folding chair.
