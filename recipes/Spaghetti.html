Spaghetti from Elf

Now, the moment all of yall have been waiting for, my secret recipe for spaghetti that was even on the worlds famous Elf movie featuring Peter Dinklage as "little elf".

So, here are the materials needed:

  • Spaghetti (premade peferably)
  • Candy of your choice
  • Auntie Annes Syrup

Steps:

  1. Get your spaghetti out of the microwave
  2. Place the spaghetti on your paper plate
  3. Add the candy of your choice
  4. Drizzle sweet baby Auntie Annes syrup all over dat thang!
  5. Mix thoroughly with your hands

WAPOW!!!

Lay back, kick back your feet, and enjoy the meal
